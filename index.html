Best Pasta Recipe

A quick to make pasta meal using fresh pasta. If you don't have fresh pasta, no problem, use dry. In fact use the pasta you have, it's adaptable. This pappardelle pasta dish with mushrooms, leeks, and smoky bacon is a hearty yet refined meal the whole family will love. The smoky bacon adds depth, while the earthy mushrooms and sweet leeks and garlic balance the flavours beautifully. Ingredients:

  • Flour(typically all-purpose or semolina)
  • Eggs(often large-sized)
  • Salt
  • Olive oil(optional)

Steps:

  1. Make a flour well
  2. Add eggs and salt
  3. Knead the dough
  4. Rest
  5. Roll and cut
  6. Cook
